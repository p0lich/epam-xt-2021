To move use arrow keys

There are some objects in game:

H - playable character. Has 3 hp
< - enemy (move only left, 5 in total)
O - collectable bonus (5 in total)
¤ - obstacle (10 in total). Player cant move over obstacle. Enemies can jamp over it
* - game bound. Player cant move over bound. Enemy start from other side of map when reach the bound

Rules:
Collect all bonuses to win. Each bonus give you 100 points. When you hit the enemy you lose 1 hp. When you hp drop to 0 you lose.
